NEEDS FARMS LLC.

Needs Farms has been delivering the highest quality straw and hay east of the mississippi river since 2009. Since its conceivement, Needs Farms has continiously made strides to grow and take advantage of cutting edge technology. We are one of a couple facilities with exclusive ownership of (blank) rebaling system. We have invested in better storage solution, bale handling, and logistical planning to ensure our product is of the highest quality as it leaves our facility to yours.

Despite the overwhelming commitment the Owner of Needs farms still invests in his passion for livestock by breeding high quality show pigs. A 30 year long resume in agriculture provides for the best quality of life for the best quality fairfield county bred showpigs. Every possible precaution was taken into consideration when building our breeding facility with a heavy emphasis on clean feeding and the perfect sterile enviroment for AI. There is no stone left unturned when Needs Farms is a player at the table and we extend the same virgor and ethical practice to you as you support our buisness.
